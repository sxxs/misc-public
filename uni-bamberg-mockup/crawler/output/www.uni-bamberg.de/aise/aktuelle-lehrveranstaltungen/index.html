Seminaristischer Unterricht

V: Computational Metaphysics - Mechanizing Principia Logico-Metaphysica (AISE-PLM)
2,00 SWS
Christoph Benzmüller , Daniel Kirchner , Edward N. Zalta
The course will be by the 2025 Johann-von-Spix Guestprofessor Dr. Edward Zalta (Stanford), Dr. Daniel Kirchner and Prof. Dr. Christoph Benzmüller. It will be held as a block course from Novemver 27 to December 5 in combination also with a workshop and the 2025 Kurt-Gödel-Essay-Award ceremony event at U Bamberg (on December 5). Topics for follow-up thesis projects will be offered to successful participants.
