Bulpros Consulting AG

Bulpros Consulting AG mit Sitz in Sofia, Bulgarien ist seit Herbst 2012 Kooperationspartner im Rahmen des Corporate Internship Programs.

Eine ausführliche Unternehmenspräsentation (Stand Sommer 2014) liegt beim Career Center WIAI und kann auf Nachfrage eingesehen werden. Sollten Sie sich für ein Praktikum bei der Firma interessieren, nehmen Sie bitte mit dem Career Center WIAI Kontakt auf.

The Office Premises
The offices of BulPros in Sofia are situated in the Business Park Sofia. It is the largest office park in Southeastern Europe and is a totally new way of doing business. The park is designed to provide efficiency, flexibility and convenience to corporate tenants. It is a genuine multifunctional high-tech business park, which combines essential components of business and related sectors in a vibrant community.

Business Park Sofia is located at the foot of Vitosha Mountain with easy access to all major road arteries and direct access to the Sofia Ring Road. The Park is less than 20 minutes away from the central business district and within 10-minute drive to the international airport.
The transport of Business Park Sofia is provided by public transport, shuttle buses, а subway station connecting the Park with the city and free transport to the last subway station for the employees working in the Park.

Living Conditions and Lifestyle

Average rents
The average studio flat rent is 150 EUR per month + utilities expenses depending on the location.

The population of Sofia consists mainly of young people. A number of restaurants and cafes operate 24 hours.

The Sofia region is also famous for its close connection to the Mount Vitosha, which is 1,800m (5,905ft) above sea level. Only 40 minutes away from the city centre, easy to get by public transport, it is perfect for skiing in winter and trekking in summer. The ski runs are of various lengths and difficulties, with chair and drag lifts and a gondola lift.

The most famous Bulgaria’s ski-resorts Bansko and Borovetz are also close to the capital – you can reach them easily by car for 1 to 2 hours.

Sofia is also close to the seaside – approximately 4 hours by car.

Bulpros Consulting will support you in all necessary matters of finding and arranging your accomodation, travel and admininistrative registrations. We will provide you with a local mentor, who will facilitate your induction in the company and the country itself. Apart from assuring a variable and interesting work experience, we will make sure that you get to know the country, local atmosphere and lifestyle. We are sure that you will enjoy the rich social and cultural life in Bulgaria and will find multiple recreational indoor and outdoor activities after a fulfilling workweek. If you are interested in the Bulgarian language, we will assist you with finding a suitable language course for the duration of your stay.

Discription
BulPros offers a wide range of IT development and infrastructure services in diverse technologies and thus variable fields of activity. We are open to provide you with an internship in the fields you are most interested in: the world of IT and project management, marketing, sales or back office.

Special Requirements
You will sign an employee contract with our company that will be registered officially, if you stay longer than 3 months with us. In such case, you will be registered as a long term foreign resident. Our HR department will assist and accompany you in all administrative matters.
