JCommerce SA

JCommerce SA is a software development and information technology outsourcing company that since 2005 specializes in nearshore software development servicesfor European customers (currently in Belgium, Germany, Great Britain, Netherlands, Finland, Denmark, Italy, Norway, and Switzerland). 

We offer software and application development services that meet the most cutting-edge technical proficiency requirements. We provide services to the representatives of the following economy sectors: IT, telecommunications, banking, finance and insurance, manufacturing, trade, services and public administration, tourism, medicine, while our offer includes:  Business Intelligence systems, portal systems, mobile SFA systems and customized solutions. We recommend in particular our IT outsourcing services in .NET, JAVA, PHP, Business Intelligence technologies and mobile development in the field of System Architecture, Enterprise System Development, Quality Assurance.

JCommerce SA co-operates with the world leaders of modern IT technologies, such as: IBM, Microsoft, SAP, QlikTech and Dell. Thanks to this we offer software that is technologically sophisticated and ideally suited to our clients’ requirements and expectations.

According to the latest edition of the Polish Computerworld TOP 200 report, JCommerce SA belongs to the group of dynamically developing Polish companies in the IT sector. The highest appreciation is deserved for the 6th place in the ranking of mobile application producers, the 8th place on the list of Web application and portal providers, and the 10th place in the ranking of the largest suppliers of Business Intelligence systems in Poland in 2011.

JCommerce SA is a company which is constantly expanding and discovers new possibilities. Our aim is to support and to develop IT talents all over the world. For the best students we offer internships in our headquarters in the capital of Silesia region, Katowice.

In JCommerce SA every employee has got an opportunity to work with the latest technologies and the best programs. If you are interested in Developing, Testing or the Business Intelligence area and you want to discover a new country and its culture you can make IT with us!

Main areas where you can improve your skills are:

  • Business Intelligence
  • WEB
  • Testing

Our requirements:

  • Computer science student
  • Engagement
  • Passion and knowledge about selected technology
  • Good level of English
  • Sense of humor
