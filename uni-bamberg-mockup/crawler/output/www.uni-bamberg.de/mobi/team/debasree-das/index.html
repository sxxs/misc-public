  • Ubiquitous Computing
    • Human Computer Interaction
    • Pervasive Sensing

Teaching

Mobile Software Systems Seminar Course (Mobi-Sem-B WiSe 25/26)

Thesis Offerings

I am interested in supervising thesis on the below topics

  1. Spatio-temporal data anonymization
  2. Contextual analysis of mobility data
  3. Ensuring safety and better availability of micromobility (e-scooters, bikes, bicycles) services

No more places for thesis supervision for the WiSe 2025/26.

Current Thesis and Project Supervision

  • Master's Thesis: Rahat Rafiq, "Balancing Privacy versus Utility: A Trade-off Analysis of Modality-Based Anonymization on Human Mobility Data". (Awarded). Related Publication: Link
  • Master's Thesis: Bishal Bhandari, "On-Demand Bus Stops for Effective Transportation" (Awarded). Related Publication: Link
  • Master's Thesis: Leveraging LLMs for Causal Reasoning in Multimodal Sensor-Based Prediction (Ongoing)
  • Master's Thesis: Privacy-Preserving Demand Prediction in Multimodal Urban Transport Systems (Ongoing)
  • Master's Thesis: Urban Intelligence-Driven Geospatial Modeling for Adaptive Micro-Mobility Rebalancing (Ongoing)
  • Project "Urban Data Platform for the City of Bamberg," emphasizing urban dynamics such as mobility, environmental conditions, and human demographics.
