Chair of Mobile Systems

Welcome to the website of the Chair of Mobile Systems and the research group "MOBI". It was founded in April 2014 within the Faculty of Information Systems and Applied Computer Sciences at the University of Bamberg.

The MOBI group is led by Prof. Dr. Daniela Nicklas and focuses on Data Management for Mobile Systems, Data Stream Management/ Complex Event Processing and Development Support for sensor-based Applications, in the area of Smart Cities.
