Regulations and Documents

Regulations of the study programme

The following documents form the formal framework for the course of studies B.Sc. Applied Computer Science:

  • The General Examination Regulations (APO) of the Faculty WIAI.
  • The study and subject examination regulations (StuFPO) for the course of studies.

The study and subject examination regulations define, among other things, the credit points (ECTS points) to be earned in the module groups. In addition, these regulations govern which courses can be attended within the individual module groups. Detailed descriptions of the individual modules listed can be found in the module handbook for the course of study.

 

Module manuals and module tables

The module table of the course of studies regulates which modules from the module handbook can be selected in the individual module groups shown in the subject examination regulations. Supplementary information and, if necessary, references to additional short-term courses can be found in the information of the Examination Board for Applied Computer Science.
