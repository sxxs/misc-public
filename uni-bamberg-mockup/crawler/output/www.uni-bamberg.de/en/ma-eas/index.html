Master in English and American Studies: two branches

Welcome to the University of Bamberg's Master in English and American Studies!

In the winter term 2013/14, we introduced an exclusively English-taught MA programme which can be studied in two branches, the "regular track" as well as the "Joint Master's Programme in English and American Studies" branch. On this page and its subpages, you will find all relevant information regarding the structure of the programme(s), the application procedures, your contact persons plus a detailed page on important appointments upon commencement of the programmes.

Both study branches at one glance (for mobile use rotate your screen sideways)

Career Day 2025

Join us for the Career Day 2025 in U7/01.05 on July 4 at 9 am. Hear from alumni of the programme and from a variety of experts about career and funding opportunities.

Joint Programme applications for 2026!

The application period for the European Joint Master's Programme in English and American Studies for the academic year 2026/27 runs from December 10, 2025 to March 26, 2026 (23:59 CET)!
