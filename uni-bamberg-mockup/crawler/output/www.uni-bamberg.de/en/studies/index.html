Live Your Dream!

Studying in Bamberg means living in a lively and at the same time relaxed university town with a historical spirit and a distinctive cultural offering. Those who choose one of the approximately 100 Bachelor's, Master's or teaching degree programmes will encounter a time-honoured educational institution with a long tradition and personal atmosphere. The charm of the historic city attracts many prospective students, for Bamberg is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful cities in Germany and has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1993. The university's facilities, on the other hand, are ultra-modern. 

With more than 350 partner universities, the University of Bamberg maintains contacts all over the world and almost 1,200 international students from around 90 countries create an open-minded and internationally oriented atmosphere in the Lectures.

Come to Bamberg and become part of our international community! Live your dream ... We look forward to meeting you!
